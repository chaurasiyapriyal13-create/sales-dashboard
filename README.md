# Sales Dashboard

## Overview
This project is a **Sales Dashboard** created to analyze and visualize sales performance in an interactive and meaningful way.  
The dashboard helps in understanding key business metrics such as **total sales, profit, quantity sold, top-performing products, regional performance, and sales trends over time**.

It is designed to support better decision-making by turning raw sales data into clear and actionable insights.

---

## Objectives
- Track overall sales performance
- Monitor profit and revenue trends
- Identify top-selling products and categories
- Compare sales across different regions/markets
- Understand customer and product behavior
- Provide data-driven insights for business growth

---

## Features
- **Total Sales Analysis** – View the overall revenue generated
- **Profit Analysis** – Measure profit performance across products and regions
- **Sales Trend Visualization** – Observe monthly or yearly sales trends
- **Category-wise Performance** – Identify which product categories perform best
- **Region-wise Analysis** – Compare sales across different locations
- **Top Products Dashboard** – Highlight best-selling products
- **Interactive Filters** – Explore the dashboard dynamically using filters/slicers
